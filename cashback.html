💡 How to Maximise Cashback

1️⃣
Always check before buying
Open TopCashback and Cashrewards before any online purchase. Even 3% on a $300 grocery order is $9 free.
2️⃣
Compare rates across all three
TopCashback, Cashrewards and ShopBack have different rates for the same store. Takes 30 seconds to check all three.
3️⃣
Stack cashback with sale prices
Cashback works on top of existing discounts. Chemist Warehouse 50% off vitamins + 8% cashback = both apply.
4️⃣
Watch for bonus events
Cashrewards and TopCashback run 10x cashback days and EOFY specials. Sign up for email alerts from both.
5️⃣
Use for insurance & energy switching
Platforms pay $50–$150 for switching insurers or energy providers through their links. Same policy, free money.
6️⃣
Do not apply outside coupon codes
Using a third-party coupon code after clicking a cashback link usually voids the cashback. Only use codes the platform itself provides.
7️⃣
Install the browser extension
TopCashback and Cashrewards both have browser extensions that alert you automatically when you visit a store that has cashback available.
8️⃣
Do not use incognito mode
Cashback tracking uses cookies. Incognito windows clear cookies so your cashback will not be tracked. Use a normal browser window.
